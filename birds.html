Peacock

Size : 25cm x 35cm
Original : 45$ (framed) Peacocks are ground-feeders that eat insects, plants, and small creatures. There are two familiar peacock species.
The blue peacock lives in India and Sri Lanka, while the green peacock is found in
Java and Myanmar (Burma). A more distinct and little-known species, the Congo peacock,
inhabits African rain forests.

Finches

Size : 35cm x 50cm
Original : 45$ (framed)
The true finches are small to medium-sized passerine birds in the family Fringillidae. Finches have stout conical
bills adapted for eating seeds and nuts and often have colourful plumage. They <>occupy a great range of habitats where they are usually resident and do not migrate.

Owl

Original : 55$ (framed)
Owls are birds from the order Strigiformes, which includes over 200 species of mostly solitary and nocturnal birds
of prey typified by an upright stance, a large, broad head, binocular vision,
binaural hearing, sharp talons, and feathers adapted for silent flight.
